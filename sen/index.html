SEN Classrooms

  • SEN Classrooms is a series of events around software engineering education
  • The SEN Classrooms series is organised by Ana-Maria Oprescu and Vadim Zaytsev.
  • We are a part of the Working Group on Education, chaired by Bastiaan Heeren.
  • This Working Group is a part of VERSEN.
