PEM/SEM Colloquium

Organisation

  • PEM is a colloquium held at Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica at least since 1997.
  • I was organising this colloquium from January 2012 till August 2013.
  • During that time, it was a relatively stable weekly event, only occasionally deviating from the schedule or merging with other events.
  • In my design, there were two types of colloquium presentations:
    • PEM (Programming Environment Meeting) — usually an invited talk by an external expert about a topic relevant for the SWAT team, or a presentation by a SWAT member intended for a broader public.
    • SEM (Software Engineering Meeting) — an internally intended talk by members of SWAT to discuss their work in progress with colleagues.
  • The last organiser before me was Bas Basten, the next one after me was Atze van der Ploeg.
  • Information about older talks was semi-automatically extracted by me from the backups of Jurgen Vinju.
